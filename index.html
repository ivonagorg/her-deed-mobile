About the Project

We believe the women who shaped history deserve to be remembered.

Her Deed is a digital archive and living museum dedicated to the women of Europe whose stories, monuments, and achievements have been overlooked, damaged, or erased.

Starting in Skopje, we are building outward across Europe, one location at a time.
